Our Story

Chi Yatai was established in December of 2019 as a dream fulfilled primarily to serve the Chicago area through food, dessert, and bubble tea. Before we opened, we spent a long time creating all authentic recipes for our food, as well as unique and all original bubble tea offerings.

To explain our restaurant name, "Chi" means Christ in Greek [pronounced kai]; "Chi" is also short for Chicago and Chinatown. A "Yatai" is a street food stand in Japan. Our mascot is Tak the Baby Octopus Chef, designed by John and Mier, to highlight our made-from-scratch Takoyaki and infuse a fun, playful cuteness into the Chi Yatai customer experience.

In closing, John and Mier, with the help of a close friend's recipes from the streets of Osaka, Japan, desire to run the restaurant to honor the Lord and bless every customer with kindness, warmth, hospitality, and love. We are humbled and grateful that you would choose Chi Yatai in Chinatown's Richland Food Court, and we hope to see you soon!

Chi Yatai成立于2019年12月，它是一个梦想的实现，我们的目的主要是通过美食、甜点和奶茶为芝加哥地区服务。在开业之前，我们花了非常多的时间研制地道的食物配方，以及独创 的奶茶饮品。

很多人好奇我们餐厅名字的由来，“ Chi”在希腊语中意为基督[发音为kai]； “ Chi”也是芝加哥(Chicago)和唐人街(Chinatown)的缩写。 “ Yatai”是日语中日本街头的小吃摊。我们的吉祥物是由约翰和觅尔设计的Tak章鱼厨师宝宝，以彰显我们从头到尾手工制作的章鱼烧，并为Chi Yatai的客户体验注入有趣、俏皮的可爱感。

最后，约翰和觅尔，在来自日本大阪街头的密友所提供的食谱的帮助下，渴望通过这家餐厅来荣耀上帝，并愿以友善，温暖，好客和爱来祝福每一位顾客。您选择唐人街置富美食广场的Chi Yatai使我们感激并受宠若惊，我们希望很快能见到您！

About Us

Mission Statement

We genuinely love our customers, we are blessed to have the best customers in all of Chicago, we want to help those in need and make a positive difference in Chicago through every dish, dessert, and drink we offer.

我们真诚地爱我们的客户，也因拥有芝加哥最好的客户群体而感受到祝福。我们想要为有需要的人提供帮助，并且通过我们提供的每道菜、甜点和饮品为芝加哥带来积极的改变。



Chi Yatai Menu Philosophy:

Chi Yatai was established to serve authentic Japanese Street Fare--made-from-scratch Takoyaki, Katsu Sando, Chicken Karaage, and Donburi made with Japan's #1-rated Koshihikari rice--as well as Organic Bubble Tea. We invite you to taste the difference at Chinatown's only restaurant using the finest hormone-free, antibiotic-free organic milk in all our bubble tea drinks. We hope you'll love that we use all-natural pure cane sugar, non-GMO, all-natural brown sugar, and fresh, all-natural ingredients.

Chi Yatai菜单理念：

Chi Yatai的成立是为要为您呈上正宗的日本路边小吃——手工章鱼烧，三明治，炸鸡、用日本排名第一的越光大米做的丼饭以及有机珍珠奶茶。我们邀请您来品尝我们不同的奶茶风味，因为我们是唐人街唯一一家在所有的奶茶中使用上好有机牛奶的店，无激素无抗生素。 并且我们希望您会喜欢我们使用的全天然的纯蔗糖，非转基因全天然的红糖以及其他新鲜天然的用料。
